NEWS

We would like to extend our heartiest congratulations to Team Raya for emerging as the winner of ‘Most Impactful Game Design’ for their Role-Playing Game (RPG) RAYA at Games Bagus, recently. Four MMU students from the 10 members of Team Raya were Muhammad Hafizuddin Hayat Abd Rani from the Faculty of Management (FOM); Goh Wei Hing from the Faculty of Creative Multimedia (FCM); Nur Sabrina Abdul Malik (FCM) and Neo Zhi Ming from the Faculty of Computing and Informatics (FCI). The team received USD 1,200 with 3 months of mentorship by a social impact mentor (Ms. Suraya Zainuddin from Ringgit Oh Ringgit) and a games industry mentor (Ms. Saqina Latif from Persona Theory Games) and a booth slot in LEVEL UP KL organised by Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC) in October 2022.
